This is the EcoTroy Map!

The EcoTroy map has been designed to help visitors find recycle bins around campus.

Environmentally conscious college students at Troy University struggle with the lack of efficient lifestyle changes on campus. While our school lacks a lot of resources agriculturally to be able to compost easily and get rid of unnecessary waste, what we do not lack are recycle bins! Hidden like a geocache around Troy University are dozens of recycle bins that are just waiting to be discovered! Feeling guilty for buying a single-use plastic water bottle? Dump it guilt free in one of the convenient recycle locations across campus. Don’t see your favorite “blue bin”? Let us know by registering your bin today!
